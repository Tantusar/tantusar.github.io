I Know What You Did

by the Admin

"I know what you did."
Oh yeah? What's that?
Did I kill someone with a baseball bat?
Did I eat ice cream until I got fat?
Did I use glossy paper instead of matte,
For an essay which I didn't hand back?
No? Huh, well how about that!

"I know what you did."
Oh yeah? How so?
Did I upset someone with a word like "no"?
Did I hurt someone with a ball of snow?
Did I make up the answers I didn't know,
Then act like it was all for show?
No? Huh, well whaddaya know?

"I know what you did."
Oh yeah? Do tell.
Did I rig explosives to the door bell?
Did I make your life a living hell?
Did I fill your shoes with an acid gel,
Then forget to ring the old death knell?
No? Huh, I thought that would go well.

"I know what you did."
Yes, so do I.
I rigged my phone to daily sigh,
Those words that are so very sly,
And it'll go on to the day I die,
I'm telling you, I cannot lie.
No? Huh, I've no reply,
To your disbelief. Good day! Good bye!
